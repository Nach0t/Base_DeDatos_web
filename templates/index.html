Welcome to the Course Management Application

This application allows you to manage courses, add students, assign professors, and track student grades and attendance. Below are the main features of this platform:

  • Add and manage university courses.
  • Assign professors to courses.
  • Add students to courses and record their evaluations.
  • Register and monitor student attendance.
  • Automatically calculate each student's average based on evaluations.
